About Me

Early Personal Development

Growing up in Wisconsin, I developed a deep passion for writing, storytelling, and visual media at an early age. I found myself drawn to the way stories could be conveyed through both words and visuals.

Throughout my school years, I honed my skills in writing and video editing, blending creativity with technical expertise. I also nurtured my passion for journalism, always seeking out new ways to inform, engage, and entertain through the power of narrative. These early experiences shaped the foundation of my career today.

I am described by my peers as creative, dedicated, and collaborative. I dive deep into the subjects that fascinate me, constantly pushing myself to learn more and improve my craft. While I thrive on solo projects, I also find fulfillment in working within a team, believing that great stories are built through collaboration and mutual respect.

Journalism and Writing

After earning my bachelor's degree in journalism from the University of Wisconsin, Madison, I began building a career in writing, editing, and storytelling. Whether reporting on local events or crafting in-depth feature pieces, my goal is always to create impactful stories that resonate with audiences.

As a writer and journalist, I’ve developed a versatile skill set, ranging from conducting interviews and researching complex topics to writing clear and engaging content for a variety of platforms. I’m passionate about bringing stories to life and using journalism as a tool for positive change.

Videography and Visual Storytelling

My passion for videography has driven me to explore the art of visual storytelling. From conceptualizing ideas to handling all aspects of video production, I aim to create compelling and visually captivating content. I love the process of transforming ideas into visuals that communicate emotion and meaning.

I’ve worked on a wide range of projects, including short documentaries, event videos, and promotional content. Each project challenges me to find innovative ways to tell stories through the lens, using a mix of technical skills and creativity.

Other Interests and Future Goals

Outside of journalism and videography, I enjoy exploring the world through travel and new experiences. My future goals include working abroad, where I can combine my passion for storytelling with my desire to document diverse cultures and perspectives.

I’m excited to continue growing as a journalist and visual storyteller, always pushing myself to create content that informs, inspires, and engages.
